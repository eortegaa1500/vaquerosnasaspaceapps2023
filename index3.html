The rain here is sulfuric, which is why clouds have a light
yellow hue and why the surface water is undrinkable.

The planet has a strong magnetic field and it finds itself
in an inversion stage, which is why we get to observe auroras
far away from the geographic poles.

Single-cell organisms present phosphorescence.
They have evolved to capture photons during
the day and re-emit them during the long nights.

Thanks to metamorphism, it is possible
to extract useful metals from the mountains.

There is an important presence of Sulphur
in the atmosphere, which makes the clouds yellow.

The ground is black, due to the presence of
silicon powder in the planet's crust.

volcanoes, rivers and glaciers. It is believed this is what

which makes the vegetation a nice blue color.
